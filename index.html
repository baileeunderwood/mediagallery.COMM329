Concery Photography

Concert Photography can be hard to shoot. You need to get the ISO just right while maintaining a high shutter speed to capture all of the action happening on stage.

In order to make sure shots come out as clear as possible, play around with the ISO and appature until you like the looks of the shots. You can always fix lighting in post edits. To be safe I always suggest shooting some in auto to have a backup.
